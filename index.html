About Me

I’m Aneudy Adames, a New York–based Full-Stack Web Developer II at BlueOps LLC. I specialize in scalable, high-performance web applications with a strong focus on user experience, accessibility, and maintainable code. I contribute across NotaryLive and StateVitalRecords as Lead Partner Integration, overseeing partner collaborations and driving integrations that support business growth and operational efficiency.

My core stack includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Vue.js on the front end, and Laravel on the back end. When projects require it, I also leverage React, Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL to build everything from responsive UIs to data-driven APIs.

From customer service work, I bring clear communication with stakeholders, solid documentation, and a habit of catching edge cases before they reach users.

I’m open to full-time roles, contract work, and collaborations on web apps, APIs, and front-end–heavy products. Let’s connect if you’d like to talk.
